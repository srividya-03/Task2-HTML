Our Story

Meet Glen Williams

Glen Williams, the founder of Glen's Bakehouse, is quiet and intense with an unwavering commitment to creating culinary masterpieces. His journey started four decades ago with Sweet Chariot which was one of the first bakeries dishing out sweet treats like Japanese cakes, Black Forest cakes, and Lemon Tarts just to name a few. Glen was the man behind Sweet Chariot's success and became a household name with over 35 outlets across Bangalore.

Glen has carefully crafted the Glen's Bakehouse offerings - whether it's a delicious cupcake, a refreshing sherbet, or rich, thick milkshake to an aromatic latte or a beautifully layered birthday cake, or a cheesy sourdough pizza from the signature firewood oven to deliriously delicious kinds of pasta to big juicy burgers to creamy pastries, Glen has ensured that every item is made from the best and freshest ingredients.

Even though Glen's Bakehouse has many branches in Bangalore, Mysore, and, now Chennai, Glen personally oversees quality and production to ensure that every customer experience has a delightful experience.

Glen Williams started his journey 40 years ago in 1981 and has been part of the success of not only Glen's Bakehouse but also Sweet Chariot and Toit.




                 

Glen's Bakehouse Story

At Glen's Bakehouse, everything is made from scratch using only the finest and freshest ingredients. Our menu is extensive and includes a wide range of bread, pastries, cakes, cookies, and other baked treats, fast food like pizzas, burgers, hot dogs, and pastas and main courses. Whether you're looking for a classic sourdough loaf, a flaky croissant, or a decadent chocolate cake, Glen's Bakehouse has got you covered.

What sets Glen's Bakehouse apart is its commitment to quality and tradition. We use traditional baking methods and recipes that have earned us the love of foodies, ensuring that every product is made with the same care and attention to detail as it was when the bakery first opened its doors.

The atmosphere at Glen's Bakehouse is warm and welcoming, with friendly staff who are always happy to help customers find the perfect treat. The bakery is known for its cozy seating area, where customers can enjoy a cup of coffee and a pastry while watching the bakers at work in the open kitchen.

Overall, Glen's Bakehouse is known for its delicious baked goods, warm hospitality, and commitment to quality and tradition. Whether you're a regular customer or a first-time visitor, you're sure to be charmed by the delicious smells and friendly faces at Glen's Bakehouse.
